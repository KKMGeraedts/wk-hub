# Knockout Prediction Result Includes Extra Time but Not Penalties

Knockout Stage score predictions are judged against the score after extra time when extra time is played, but penalty shootout goals are never added to the Prediction Result. If the Knockout Stage Prediction Result is still a draw after extra time, participants choose a separate Advancing Team; bracket progression and knockout outcome scoring also resolve from Advancing Team instead of inferring advancement from the predicted or scored result. API-Football exposes this shape for penalty-decided matches through `status.short = PEN`, `goals` as the after-extra-time score, `score.penalty` as shootout evidence, and `teams.*.winner` as the Advancing Team. This matches how participants reason about knockout ties while keeping penalty shootouts out of football score totals.
